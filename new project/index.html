Welcome to the World

Korea - Seoul

Seoul is a bustling metropolis known for its modern skyscrapers, pop culture, and historical palaces.

Turkey - Istanbul

Istanbul, a city that bridges Europe and Asia, is famous for its rich history, stunning mosques, and vibrant bazaars.

China - Beijing

Beijing, the capital of China, is home to the Great Wall, Forbidden City, and rich cultural heritage.

Japan - Tokyo

Tokyo is a high-tech city with historic temples, neon-lit streets, and a love for anime and sushi.

Kazakhstan - Almaty

Almaty, the largest city in Kazakhstan, is famous for its breathtaking mountains and cultural diversity.

France - Paris

Paris, the City of Lights, is known for the Eiffel Tower, exquisite cuisine, and world-renowned museums.

Dubai - Safari

Dubai is a futuristic city known for its luxurious lifestyle, skyscrapers, and desert adventures.
